Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers
to Common Questions

How do I download and install KrishiMitra?

You can download KrishiMitra APK directly from our website or scan the QR code. The app supports Android 6.0 and above. For installation help, contact us on WhatsApp.

Is KrishiMitra free to use?

Yes! KrishiMitra is completely free for all farmers. Our mission is to democratize access to modern farming technology across India.

Which languages does the app support?

KrishiMitra supports 12+ Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi, and many more. You can easily switch languages in the app settings.

Do I need internet connection to use the app?

Many features work offline including crop disease detection, weather forecasts (cached), and educational content. However, real-time data and community features require internet connection.
