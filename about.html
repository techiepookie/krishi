Our Mission
Transforming Agriculture

KrishiMitra was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern technology. We believe that every farmer deserves access to the latest agricultural innovations, regardless of their location or resources.

Developed under the innovative umbrella of Anam AI, our platform combines artificial intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), and machine learning to create comprehensive solutions for modern farming challenges.

From soil health monitoring to crop disease detection, from weather prediction to financial planning, KrishiMitra serves as a complete digital companion for farmers across India.
